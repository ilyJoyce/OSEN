Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

The CommUNIX Collective believes privacy is a fundamental right. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Website Usage: We collect anonymous analytics data to improve our website. This includes:

  • Pages visited
  • Referring sites
  • Browser and device information

Downloads: We do not track or record individual downloads of CommUNIX ISOs.

2. Information We Don't Collect

We deliberately avoid collecting:

  • IP addresses (they are anonymized before processing)
  • Personal identifiers
  • Location data beyond country-level

3. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Matomo Analytics (self-hosted, data never leaves our servers)
  • Forums and Git hosting (self-hosted whenever possible)

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking

5. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this policy will be voted on by the community and announced prominently on our website.

Code of Conduct

Our Community Standards

The CommUNIX community is committed to creating a welcoming environment for all, regardless of background or identity. All participants must abide by this Code of Conduct.

Expected Behavior

All community members should:

  • Be respectful and inclusive
  • Collaborate openly
  • Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully
  • Show empathy toward others

Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

  • Harassment or discrimination of any kind
  • Trolling or personal attacks
  • Promotion of proprietary software in our spaces
  • Disrespecting community decisions made through democratic processes

Enforcement

Violations will be addressed by the elected Conduct Committee through a transparent process. Consequences may include:

  • Warning
  • Temporary suspension from community spaces
  • Permanent ban in severe cases

Reporting

To report violations, email conduct@communix.org. All reports are confidential.
